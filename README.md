## What Is Protein Powder?

Protein powder is a concentrated source of protein derived from animal or plant sources. It is typically mixed with water, milk, or smoothies to create a quick and easy protein-rich drink.

### Common Types of Protein Powder:
- Whey Protein  
- Casein Protein  
- Plant-Based Protein (pea, soy, rice)  
- Egg Protein

## How to Choose the Best Protein Powder

Selecting the right protein powder is crucial for maximizing muscle gain.

### Key Factors:

**Protein Content**  
Look for 20–30 grams of protein per serving.

**Low Sugar and Additives**  
Avoid products with excessive sugar or artificial ingredients.

**Digestibility**  
Choose whey isolate or plant-based protein if you have lactose sensitivity.

**Brand Reputation**  
Always select trusted and well-reviewed brands.

## How Much Protein Do You Need?

For effective muscle gain:

- **1.6 to 2.2 grams of protein per kg of body weight per day**

Protein powder should support your diet, not replace whole food sources like meat, fish, eggs, and legumes.
